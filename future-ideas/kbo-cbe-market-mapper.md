# KBO/CBE Market Mapper

## Concept

A professional Belgian company and market-mapping tool using KBO/CBE data. It would help explore companies by sector, location, age, NACE code, legal form, and market density.

## Why It Is Interesting

- Potential B2B/professional value.
- Useful for consulting, sales targeting, local economic research, and market sizing.
- Can turn public company registry data into practical maps and lists.
- Could eventually support exports, saved searches, and lead discovery.

## Likely APIs and Data Sources

- KBO/CBE public search or bulk data where legally and technically available
- NACE code definitions
- Belgian geocoding and administrative boundaries
- Statbel municipality and sector statistics
- regional economic datasets

## Relationship to StroomMoment

It may reuse general data-ingestion, caching, and scoring patterns, but it is a different product with a different audience. It should likely remain a separate app or repository area.

## Why It Is Parked for Now

It is more B2B and business-data oriented. The first MVP should stay focused on the consumer/homelab energy timing problem.
